About

FromSoftware, Inc. is a Japanese video game development and publishing company based in Tokyo. Founded by Naotoshi Zin in November 1986, the company developed business software before releasing their first video game, King's Field, in 1994. The company would achieve breakout success in the 2010s, spurred by Demon's Souls (2009) and Dark Souls (2011), the latter being the first entry in a trilogy whose success led to the creation of a subgenre of action role-playing games known as Soulslikes. These include Bloodborne (2015), Sekiro (2019), and Elden Ring (2022).

Methodology and Principals

daring to be different

design

Most modern development companies prefer to make their games accessible to as many people as possible, which in turn means that means that most modern games offer up no challenge. from software never followed that rule, they make games that are meant to challenge the player.

Another way From Software differentiate themselves from their competitors is in the way they allow you to miss content which is in stark contrast to most game devs who seem to be afraid that the player would miss anything in the first playthrough.

Lore

The Soulsborne games are well known for their vast amounts of lore and backstory but very light storytelling. these games prefer to tell their story thorough the architecture and the environment. another way the Soulsborne games tell there story is with by giving every item a description to tie it to the world. these systems that everyone would know some of the story but to know more one would have to look.

the souls-like genre

When art imitates art

A Soulslike (also spelled Souls-like) is a subgenre of action role-playing and action-adventure games known for high levels of difficulty and emphasis on environmental storytelling, typically in a dark fantasy setting. Souls-like games gained massive success in reacent years. here are some of the best ones out there.

Remnant: the from ashes

Remnant is a slower pace shooter where you'll need to conserve your ammunition or miss as little as possible. If you like shooters and need an entry point to souls-like, This is the one.

Hollow Knight

One of the best platformers and Metroidvanias of all time. It features clean and satisfying combat, stunningly beautiful art and a massive amount of lore for you to look through.

Nioh

A medieval japanese inspired RPG with smooth and fast combat featuring a stance mechanic with each stance being effective aginst different enemy types. That and the unique setting makes a game that is hard to put down.
